We are MY Traders

Welcome to MY Traders, where we bring together the best in grocery retail and high-quality detergent manufacturing under one trusted name. With two dedicated divisions MY Supermarket and MY Products we are committed to delivering convenience, quality, and value to our customers.

MY Products specializes in the production of premium detergents. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing processes ensure that we deliver high-performing, eco-friendly cleaning solutions designed to meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

MY Supermarket is your go-to destination for fresh groceries, offering a wide range of products from locally sourced produce to everyday essentials, all at competitive prices. We pride ourselves on providing a welcoming, convenient shopping experience that meets the needs of our community.
